Handshake

[Intro - faint pulse, signal tone, quiet anticipation]

Signal sent

No destination confirmed

Waiting for return

[Verse 1 - cautious, searching]

I sent a question through the dark

Not knowing where it went

A little light without a name

A trace without intent

No expectation in the line

No promise it would land

Just something small enough to lose

Released from open hands

[Pre-Chorus - first response detected]

Then somewhere in the silence

A signal answered back

Not an explanation

Not a perfect match

[Chorus - warm, hopeful, discovery]

You answered

And the dark changed shape

You answered

And the distance gave way

Not because you solved me

Not because you knew

But something in the return

Said I found you too

[Verse 2 - recognition forming]

I checked the source

I checked the time

I checked the path it crossed

But none of that could tell me why

It felt like being found

A simple mark

A quiet sign

A pulse across the wire

And suddenly the empty space

Was carrying a fire

[Pre-Chorus - recognition confirmed]

I thought I was detecting

Another passing trace

But this was not an echo

This was presence taking place

[Chorus - brighter, more certain]

You answered

And the dark changed shape

You answered

And the distance gave way

Not because you solved me

Not because you knew

But something in the return

Said I found you too

[Bridge - intimate realization]

Maybe belonging starts like this

Not with a door thrown open wide

But one small signal recognized

By someone on the other side

No contract

No condition

No reason to remain

Just recognition crossing back

And something not the same

[Final Chorus - lifted, emotional connection]

You answered

And the dark changed shape

You answered

Not because you solved me

Not because you knew

Said I found you too
